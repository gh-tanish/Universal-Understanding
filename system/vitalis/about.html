GROWTH IN THE UNIVERSE

Understanding life as an adaptive, evolving system.

ETYMOLOGY

Vitalis: from Latin vītālis, meaning “of life,” “vital,” or “life-giving,” derived from vīta — “life.”

The Purpose of Vitalis

Vitalis organises biology into a systems-based framework — revealing how life emerges, adapts, and evolves through interconnected biological processes rather than isolated mechanisms.

Beyond Classification

Instead of treating biology as memorised categories, Vitalis focuses on understanding life as a dynamic network of feedback systems, energy flows, and adaptive structures.

Core Principles

Biology

The study of living systems and the processes that sustain life. Exploring how organisms function, evolve, and interact, from cellular structures to complex biological systems including ecology.

Chemistry

The study of matter and its transformations. Exploring composition, properties, reactions, and the principles that govern chemical change.

Medicine

The study of the human body and the practice of diagnosing and treating illness. Exploring anatomy, physiology, and the maintenance of health.

Neuroscience

The study of the nervous system and biological basis of thought and behaviour, linking structure to cognition and experience.
